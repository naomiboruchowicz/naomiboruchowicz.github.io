Privacy Policy

Seattle Date Night Carousels · Last updated June 5, 2026

Seattle Date Night Carousels (“the App”) is a personal content-publishing tool operated by its owner. The App curates publicly available information about Seattle date-night venues and publishes image carousels to the owner’s own social media accounts, including TikTok. This policy explains what data the App handles and how.

Information we access

  • Your TikTok account authorization. When you connect your TikTok account, the App receives an OAuth access token and refresh token that allow it to publish content to your account and read your basic account information (username and profile basics).
  • Content you create. The carousel images and captions the App generates and posts on your behalf.

The App does not collect, store, or process data about any other TikTok users, their content, or their interactions. It does not access your private messages, contacts, or browsing activity.

How we use information

  • To authenticate with TikTok and publish carousels to your own account.
  • To refresh your access token so scheduled posting can continue without re-authorizing each time.

How information is stored

Access and refresh tokens are stored locally in the operator’s own environment configuration and are not transmitted to any third party other than TikTok itself. We do not maintain a public database of user data and we do not sell, rent, or share personal information.

Third-party services

  • TikTok — for publishing content and authentication, governed by TikTok’s own privacy policy.
  • Image hosting (imgbb) — rendered carousel images are uploaded to a public image host so TikTok can retrieve them by URL. Only the App’s own generated images are uploaded.
  • Anthropic API — used to generate carousel copy from public venue information. No TikTok user data is sent to this service.

Data retention & deletion

Because the App stores only the operator’s own OAuth tokens locally, you can revoke its access at any time from your TikTok account settings (Manage app permissions), which immediately invalidates the stored tokens. To request deletion of any data, contact us at the address below.

Children’s privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “last updated” date above reflects the latest revision.
